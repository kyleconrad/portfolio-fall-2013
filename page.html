About

My name’s Kyle. I’m a designer. I’m sarcastic. I sing in the car. I love Chuck Taylors and Wayfarers. I’m a font nerd. I organize my closet by color. I was born on a Friday. I’ve been to the emergency room in every place I’ve ever lived.

I’m an interactive designer for nclud in Washington, DC. You should check us out – we’re nice people and we do great work.

I write about technology, web design, advertising, and creativity on AwesomeThings. I explore and experiment with web design concepts and technologies on Big Ideas. I write about music & movies on Mistaken For Strangers.

Things I like: Summer thunderstorms. Dog-eared corners and weathered pages in an old book. Post-it notes. Pictures from a plastic lens camera. Vinyl. Letterpress printing. Lists.

For fun: Exploring. Live music. Trying new food. Playing Scrabble. College football. Dance parties.

Favorite places: Black Cat and 9:30 Club in DC. New York City, Austin, Chicago, New Orleans, Sydney.

Other than that, I post random thoughts on Twitter, random things on Tumblr, photographs on Flickr and Instagram, designs on Dribbble, and occasionally make a fool of myself on Facebook.

Do you have an awesome project that needs my design skills? I’m available for limited freelance work, so check out my resume or email me.
