The After Party

An Event Apart is an annual design conference for “people who make websites,” founded by A List Apart as a way for speakers and web people to mingle, network, and learn about their industry. For the 2013 conference held in Washington, DC, nclud was proud to be a sponsor of the conference as well as the host to the closing after party. Based on an art gallery, the “Event Aparty” website was designed to be modern with a hint of old world class and touches of humor laced throughout (and fully responsive, of course). Built on a Ruby On Rails backbone, the site also incorporated registration via a simple authorization with Twitter, then displaying the attendees’ pictures in the final “gallery.”
